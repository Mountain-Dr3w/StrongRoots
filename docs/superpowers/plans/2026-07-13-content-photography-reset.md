# Strong Roots Content and Photography Reset

## Intent

Keep the Loam visual system and existing page structure, but replace the generic fitness-site message with a clear training-practice position: Strong Roots helps people build strength they can sustain through structured programs or ongoing coaching with Ashlyn.

## Content Direction

The homepage should establish the outcome and point of view before listing products. Present two offers: self-directed programs and ongoing coaching. Nutrition remains a supporting part of selected coaching tiers, not a standalone third offer. Replace vague phrases such as "what's on offer," "monthly conversation," and "trains people online" with concrete descriptions of programming, feedback, adaptation, and long-term progress.

The training-block receipt remains the proof anchor, but it must be framed as a transparent example of how progress is reviewed, not as implied client evidence. About-page language should explain Ashlyn's coaching method without inventing credentials, biography, or a target demographic that the repository does not establish.

## Photography Direction

Remove the unrelated stock people and the image labeled as Ashlyn. Until real photography is supplied, use one cohesive editorial set focused on the work itself: loading a bar, recording a training block, reviewing form, and the quiet details of a working gym. Faces, visible brand logos, transformation imagery, staged group fitness, food flat-lays, and imagery that implies a false identity are out of scope.

## Scope

Update homepage, about, catalog, product-detail, booking, global metadata, and footer copy where the current message conflicts with this direction. Replace stock references across public marketing routes with the new editorial assets. Preserve the current component architecture, color system, typography, commerce behavior, and database schema.

## Verification

Run lint and a production build, inspect desktop and mobile screenshots of the homepage and about page, confirm that no marketing route references the old stock assets, and check the final diff for unsupported claims or invented personal details.
